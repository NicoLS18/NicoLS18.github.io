Plan Your Visit

Make the most of your day at Wildwood Zoo with our helpful planning guide. Whether you're visiting for the first time or you're a seasoned zoo-goer, we have tips and information to ensure you have an amazing experience exploring our 75-acre park and meeting our animal residents.

Visitor Tips

We recommend arriving early in the day when animals are most active, especially during warmer months. Many of our species are more energetic in the morning hours before the heat of the day sets in. Comfortable walking shoes are essential, as our zoo covers extensive grounds with varied terrain including hills and natural pathways.

Don't forget to bring sunscreen, hats, and water bottles, especially during summer visits. While we have water fountains throughout the zoo, staying hydrated is important for enjoying your full day. We also have several dining options ranging from quick snacks to full meals, but you're welcome to bring your own food and use our designated picnic areas.

To enhance your experience, we offer audio guides available at the entrance, as well as a mobile app that provides interactive maps, animal information, and real-time updates about feeding times and special presentations. Our staff members stationed throughout the zoo are always happy to answer questions and provide recommendations based on your interests and the time you have available.

Getting Here

Wildwood Zoo is conveniently located just off Highway 101 at 2500 Wildlife Way. We offer ample free parking in our main lot, with designated spaces for accessible parking near the entrance. Public transportation is available via Metro Bus Route 45, which stops directly at our front gates every 30 minutes during operating hours.
