Welcome to Wildwood Zoo

Discover the wonders of wildlife at Wildwood Zoo, where conservation meets education. Our zoo is home to over 200 species from six continents, providing visitors with unforgettable experiences and inspiring a passion for protecting our planet's incredible biodiversity.

Why Visit Wildwood Zoo?

At Wildwood Zoo, we believe in creating meaningful connections between people and animals. Our world-class facilities provide naturalistic habitats that prioritize animal welfare while offering visitors incredible viewing opportunities. Every admission ticket directly supports our conservation programs working to protect endangered species in the wild.

From the majestic African savanna exhibit to our immersive rainforest biodome, each area of the zoo has been carefully designed to educate and inspire. Our dedicated team of keepers, veterinarians, and educators work tirelessly to ensure every animal receives exceptional care while sharing their knowledge with our guests through daily talks, feeding demonstrations, and interactive programs.

Zoo Highlights

  • African Savanna - Watch elephants, giraffes, and lions in our expansive 5-acre habitat
  • Rainforest Biodome - Walk through a climate-controlled tropical paradise with free-flying birds and butterflies
  • Penguin Cove - Experience underwater viewing of our playful penguin colony during daily feeding times
  • Children's Zoo - Get up close with friendly farm animals and learn about domestic species
  • Conservation Center - Discover our breeding programs for endangered species
